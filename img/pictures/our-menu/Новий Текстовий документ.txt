Latte
A latte is a coffee drink with espresso, steamed milk and a layer of foam on top. It has ⅓ espresso and 2/3 steamed milk, with a thin layer of foam on top.
$3.25

Americano
An Americano is an espresso drink made with hot water and espresso. The drink can be made with either one or two shots of espresso, and varying ratios of water (usually 2:1).
$2.62

Macchiato
A caffè macchiato or espresso macchiato is a shot of espresso that’s topped with frothed milk.
$3.50

Cappuccino
A cappuccino is an espresso drink with steamed milk, milk foam and espresso. It has equal parts espresso, steamed milk and foam (⅓ each).
$4.36

Espresso Con Panna
A coffee drink with a single or double shot of espresso topped with a dollop of whipped cream.
$2.85
